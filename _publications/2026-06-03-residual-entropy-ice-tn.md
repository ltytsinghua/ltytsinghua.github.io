We analyze the normality of the transfer matrices for hexagonal and cubic ices, and
show numerically that these transfer matrices are close to normal. On this basis, using
high-precision tensor-network methods that encode the ice rule explicitly into local
tensors, we provide numerical evidence that the residual entropy of hexagonal ice equals
that of cubic ice.
