We develop a Ginzburg-Landau theory for commensurate pair-density-wave states in
hexagonal lattice systems relevant to the kagome superconductors AV3Sb5. Mapping the
superconducting fluctuations onto an effective J1-J2 frustrated XY model on the
emergent kagome lattice and solving it with a state-of-the-art tensor-network method,
we confirm a vestigial charge-6e condensed phase, providing a potential explanation
for the charge-6e magnetoresistance oscillations observed in recent experiments.
