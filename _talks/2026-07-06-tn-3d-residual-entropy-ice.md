Invited seminar at the Quantum Science Center of Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area (host: Dezhang Li).

On extending tensor-network methods to three-dimensional classical statistical models: a split corner-transfer-matrix renormalization group (split CTMRG) scheme for triple-layer contraction, benchmarked on the 3D Ising model, and its application to the long-standing question of whether the residual entropies of hexagonal and cubic ice are equal.
