The first piece in my "羽记·造物" series — small things I vibe-coded with AI.

This is the classic Snake, with a twist. Switch to **math mode** and a question appears
at the top of the screen while numbered food is scattered on the map — eat the correct
answer to score, eat a wrong one and it's game over. Questions cover high-school topics:
trigonometry, vector dot/cross products, angles between vectors, projections. Answer
enough and the map grows a ring larger. Three difficulty levels (Easy / Hard / Nightmare)
and a leaderboard round it out.
